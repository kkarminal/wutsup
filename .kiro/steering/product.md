# Wutsup — Product Overview

## Purpose

Wutsup is a mobile application that helps users discover food, restaurants, deals, promotions, events, and local activities in their area. It serves as a centralized hub where people can find what's happening around them, from daily lunch specials to weekend festivals, all in one place.

## Target Users

- **Local residents** looking for nearby dining options, entertainment, and things to do
- **Visitors and tourists** exploring a new area who want curated local recommendations
- **Deal seekers** interested in promotions, discounts, and limited-time offers from local businesses
- **Event-goers** searching for upcoming events, meetups, and community activities

## Key Features

- **Restaurant and food discovery** — Browse nearby restaurants, cafes, and food trucks with menus, ratings, and hours
- **Deals and promotions** — View active deals, coupons, and special offers from local businesses
- **Event listings** — Discover local events, festivals, markets, and community gatherings
- **Local activities** — Find things to do including outdoor activities, classes, and entertainment
- **Search and browse** — Explore content by category, location, or keyword

## Filtering and Personalization

The application aims to surface the most relevant content for each user by applying filtering and personalization based on user preferences:

- **Location-based filtering** — Prioritize results near the user's current location or a chosen area
- **Category preferences** — Let users indicate interests (e.g., food, nightlife, family-friendly, outdoor) to tailor what appears in their feed
- **Dietary and cuisine preferences** — Filter restaurant and food results by cuisine type, dietary restrictions, or price range
- **Time-aware relevance** — Surface deals and events that are currently active or upcoming, deprioritizing expired or past content
- **Personalized recommendations** — Over time, learn from user interactions (views, saves, check-ins) to improve the relevance of suggested content
